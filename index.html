About
The Omnichain Fungible Token Standard (OFT) is built on top of LayerZero's innovative omnichain messaging infrastructure, which allows fungible tokens to be securely transferred across chains, without the need for asset wrapping, middlechains, or liquidity pools. This allows the tokens to remain in their native form, and the balances modified seamlessly across other networks. OFT's are issued by 1st part contracts, and allow for dApps to integrate easily with them. Learn more about OFT.
